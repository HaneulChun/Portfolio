> PROJECT_OVERVIEW

Neon Nexus is a Top Down, 2.5D, Stealth game. Where you play as the repair bot Roko, who has broken free from the AI Hive Mind. While Navigating the Maze Like floors of the Basilisk Tower you will use Roko’s Omni-Tool to temporarily Disable Patrol Bots and to Solve Puzzles while progressing to the super computer at the top of the tower in order to Destroy the AI Hive Mind and free the other robots.

> CORE_FEATURES

  • ▶AI System: Enemies patrol with vision cones and avoid obstacles
  • ▶Enemy Variety: Includes ground units, flying scouts, and cameras
  • ▶Combat: Players can stun ground enemies to slip past.

> TECHNICAL_IMPLEMENTATION

  • ▶Enemies patrol predefined paths and switch to chase mode when the player is detected
  • ▶Player detection is handled through raycast-based vision cones that check line-of-sight in real time
  • ▶Ground enemies can be stunned by player projectiles, temporarily disabling their behavior
  • ▶player detection meter that fills when in enemy vision

SWEET_SPOT.UE5

HORROR_PUZZLE_ANALYSIS
UNREAL_ENGINE C++_CORE HORROR_DESIGN PUZZLE_SYSTEMS

> PROJECT_OVERVIEW

Sweet Spot is a first-person puzzle-horror game where you play as a hotel concierge trapped in a looping hallway by an eldritch horror. Manage your Madness Meter—Sane, The Sweet Spot, and Mad. While in The Sweet Spot, use your Focus ability to reveal hidden items, solve puzzles, and find the missing elevator pieces to escape.

> CORE_FEATURES

  • ▶Focus Ability System: Unique mechanic revealing hidden environmental clues
  • ▶Atmospheric Horror: Psychological tension through environmental storytelling
  • ▶Madness Meter:Sane / Focus / Mad, with Madness increasing indoors and easing in halls

> TECHNICAL_IMPLEMENTATION

  • ▶Tentacle and eye entities that fade when looked at while in Focus mode
  • ▶Smooth camera with adjustable FOV, vignette, and chromatic aberration effects
  • ▶Objects that fade in and out of reality based on the player’s Madness
